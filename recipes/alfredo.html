Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (1 stick)
  • 2 cups grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish, optional)

Instructions:

  1. Melt Butter and Cook Garlic (Optional): In a large skillet or saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. If using garlic, add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant (be careful not to burn it).
  2. Add Heavy Cream: Pour the heavy cream into the pan and stir it into the butter. Heat the mixture, stirring occasionally, until it starts to simmer. Do not let it boil.
  3. Incorporate Parmesan Cheese: Gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese, a little at a time, until it melts and blends smoothly into the sauce.
  4. Season the Sauce: Add salt and black pepper to taste. Stir the sauce until it reaches your desired consistency (it should be creamy and slightly thick).
  5. Serve Immediately: Toss the Alfredo sauce with cooked pasta (such as fettuccine, linguine, or penne) and mix well to coat evenly. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.

    6. Tips:

    1. For a richer flavor, you can add a pinch of nutmeg or a splash of white wine while simmering the cream.
    2. Use freshly grated Parmesan for the best taste and texture.
    3. Serve immediately, as Alfredo sauce thickens as it cools.
