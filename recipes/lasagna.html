Lasagna

Ingredients:

For the meat sauce:

  • 1 pound ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the cheese filling:

  • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

For assembly:

  • 12 lasagna noodles (cooked and drained or no-boil noodles)
  • Extra mozzarella and Parmesan cheese for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:

    2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat. Cook the ground beef until browned, breaking it into small pieces.

    Add the chopped onion and garlic, cooking until softened (about 3-5 minutes).

    Stir in the marinara sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Let simmer for 10-15 minutes. Remove from heat.

  2. Make the Cheese Filling:

    3. In a medium bowl, mix ricotta cheese, 1 cup of mozzarella, Parmesan, egg, salt, and pepper until well combined.

  3. Preheat the Oven:

    4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  4. Assemble the Lasagna:

    5. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.

    Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.

    Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of meat sauce.

    Repeat the layers (noodles, cheese mixture, meat sauce) until all ingredients are used, ending with meat sauce on top.

    Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and extra Parmesan cheese on top for a golden crust.

  5. Bake:

    6. Cover the dish with aluminum foil (to prevent the cheese from burning) and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.

    Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.

  6. Rest and Serve:

    7. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing. This helps the layers set.
